Game Modes

The evil fish is everywhere, help the ocean to catch them all. They are hinding behind some of these bubbles, your mission is to discover where they are and catch them all. Good Luck!

Game instruction:

  1. Choose the size of the board
  2. Choose the difficulty level
  3. Left-mouse click for popping the bubble to expose it.
  4. Right-mouse click for catching your suspected bubble.
